## Coin Management

### Two Types of Coins

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="General Coins" icon="piggy-bank">
    Coins you purchase or earn through daily bonuses and referrals. **Never expire** and remain in your wallet permanently.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subscription Coins" icon="calendar">
    Coins included with your monthly subscription. **Expire at the end of each billing cycle** (30 days).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Smart Coin Usage

<Info>
Our system is designed to maximize your value! When you use coins, the system **automatically uses Subscription Coins first** (which expire) before touching your General Coins (which don't expire). This ensures you never waste any coins.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="If I have unlimited chats, why do I need coins?">
    Unlimited chats apply only to the models **included in your tier**. Coins let you access more advanced models and special features beyond your plan. For example, a Prime member has unlimited Titan chats but can use coins to try Sage or Nova.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I run out of coins?">
    You can still chat unlimited with all the models included in your subscription tier! You'll only need more coins if you want to use advanced models or premium features beyond your plan.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my subscription without using any coins?">
    Absolutely! If you're satisfied with the models included in your tier, you never need to use coins. Many subscribers stick to their unlimited models and save coins for special occasions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I lose my coins if I cancel my subscription?">
    No! Your **General Coins** (purchased or earned) stay in your wallet forever. Only **Subscription Coins** from your current billing cycle will expire when your subscription ends.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I gift coins to other users?">
    This feature is not currently available, but we're exploring it for future updates!
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I check my coin balance?">
    Your coin balance is always visible in your account settings and at the top of the chat interface. You can see both your General Coins and Subscription Coins separately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
